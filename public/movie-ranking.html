The best Animes according to Dabira

My top 3 Animes of all time

Death Note

This is about a high-school kid that found a notebook, called a death note, that brings to pass the death of whomever's name is written inside it.

One piece

This is about a young boy whose dream is to become the king of pirates in an era of pirates.

Naruto

This is about the journey of a boy from childhood to adulthood, his friends, his ideals, his struggles, and his triumphs in a caste-driven and war-riddled world.
